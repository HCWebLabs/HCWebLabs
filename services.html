What Affects the Final Price?

Every project is unique. Here's what influences where your project falls in the pricing range:

Number of Pages

5 pages = lower range • 20+ pages = higher range

Simple sites (Home, About, Services, Contact) cost less than complex sites with multiple service pages, team bios, case studies, and resources.

Design Complexity

Template-based = lower range • Fully custom = higher range

Using a premium template with minor customization is faster and more affordable than designing every element from scratch.

Custom Features & Integrations

Basic features = lower range • Advanced integrations = higher range

Contact forms and photo galleries are standard. Custom booking systems, membership portals, API integrations, and complex databases cost more.

Content & Copywriting

You provide content = lower range • I write everything = higher range

If you have your text, images, and branding ready, development is faster. If I'm writing copy, sourcing images, or creating graphics, it adds time and cost.

Timeline & Revisions

Standard timeline = included revisions • Rush delivery or extra revisions = additional cost

Normal turnaround times include 2-3 rounds of revisions. Expedited projects or requests for unlimited revisions will adjust the pricing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about my services, process, and pricing. Don't see your question? Feel free to reach out!

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline varies by project type. Microsites typically take 1-2 weeks, Multi-Page Websites take 2-4 weeks, E-Commerce stores need 3-5 weeks, and Website Redesigns are usually complete in 1-3 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! For projects over $750, I offer a payment plan: 50% upfront to begin work, and 50% upon completion. For larger projects over $1,500, we can arrange three payments: 40% to start, 30% at the midpoint, and 30% at launch. This keeps the project moving smoothly while being flexible for your budget.

What's included in the revision rounds?

Each package includes 2-3 rounds of revisions, which means you can request changes to design elements, content layout, colors, fonts, and functionality within the scope of your package. Revisions don't include major scope changes or new features beyond what was originally agreed upon. Additional revision rounds are available at an hourly rate if needed.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?

Absolutely! Multi-Page Websites and E-Commerce sites can include a CMS (Content Management System) that lets you update text, images, blog posts, and products yourself. I provide training and documentation so you can confidently manage your content. For Microsites, content updates are typically handled through code, but I'm always available for quick updates at my hourly rate.

What if I need ongoing support or maintenance?

Technical Support is available at $55/hour for troubleshooting, updates, security patches, and general maintenance. For ongoing needs, I offer monthly retainer packages that provide priority support, regular backups, security monitoring, and a set number of hours for updates each month. This is often more cost-effective than hourly billing for active websites.
