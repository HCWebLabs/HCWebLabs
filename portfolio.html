My Process

Every project follows a proven methodology to ensure quality, efficiency, and client satisfaction.

Discovery

We start by understanding your goals, target audience, and project requirements. This phase includes research, competitive analysis, and defining project scope.

Design

Creating wireframes and mockups that bring your vision to life. We iterate on designs until they perfectly match your brand and user needs.

Development

Building your site with clean, modern code. Focus on performance, accessibility, and responsive design across all devices and browsers.

Testing

Rigorous quality assurance across browsers, devices, and screen sizes. Testing for functionality, performance, accessibility, and user experience.

Launch

Deploying your site to production with proper optimization, SEO setup, and analytics integration. Includes training on content management if needed.

Support

Ongoing maintenance, updates, and support to keep your site running smoothly. Available for technical issues, content updates, and new features.
